Our Story

Started in Tumkur. Grown on repeat customers.

Abhishek Enterprises was founded in Tumkur, Karnataka, with one goal — to manufacture HDPE and PP woven packaging that industrial buyers could depend on without follow-up calls. Fifteen years later, that's still the standard we hold every batch to.

We've since expanded from HDPE woven sacks into PP woven bags, shrink film and custom liners, adding capacity and newer weaving technology as demand from manufacturing, chemical and food-goods customers grew across the state.

Every order that leaves our facility still gets the same attention the first one did — because most of our business today comes from customers who started with a single trial order years ago.
